About Elitommy Fruity Zobo

At Elitommy Fruity Zobo, we believe drinks should be more than just refreshing — they should be an experience.

Our journey started with a simple idea: to make the traditional Nigerian hibiscus drink (Zobo) healthier, fresher, and fruitier. Today, we proudly serve Lagos with unique blends like Pineapple Twist, Ginger Zing, and Citrus Burst.

Every bottle is brewed with 100% natural ingredients, no preservatives, and a lot of love. Whether you’re relaxing at home, hosting friends, or throwing a party, we’ve got the perfect fruity refreshment for you.

Our Mission

To redefine Zobo by making it modern, fruity, and absolutely refreshing, while keeping its rich cultural roots alive.

Why Choose Us?

  • Freshly brewed in Lagos, delivered chilled.
  • Unique fruity flavors you won’t find anywhere else.
  • Affordable packs for individuals, families, and events.
  • Friendly and fast customer service.
